Upfront Payment Schedule:

Level Total Dollars Earned* Early Payout %
1 $0-$24,999 0%
2 $25,000-$49,999 25%
3 $50,000+ 50%

*Payout begins on the first project after the payout minimum is earned. For example, if you complete a project with a total developer payout of $30,000 and then begin a second project with a total developer payout of $5,000, the early payout would be applied to the $5,000 project which is the first project after the project during which the payout minimum was earned.
